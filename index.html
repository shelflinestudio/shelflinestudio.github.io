Problem

Small Shopify brands often have product photos that work for a product page, but not for paid social. The image is clean, yet it does not show a lifestyle angle, visual hook, or reason to test the product as an ad.

  • Before: a plain white-background product photo.
  • Missing: customer context, use case, and ad variants.
  • Goal: make the product easier to understand and easier to test.

Creative Strategy

The strategy was not to invent a fake brand story. It was to take one usable product image and build a small testing system around it: lifestyle context, direct copy, utility callouts, and layout extensions for common ad placements.

3 Creative Angles

Desk Reset

Show the tray as one landing spot for the small things that keep spreading across a work surface.

Product Explainer

Use props and callouts so a cold viewer understands phone, keys, and cards in one glance.

Giftable Upgrade

Frame the product as a useful small desk gift or finishing touch for a new workspace setup.
